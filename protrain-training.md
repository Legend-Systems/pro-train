## 1. Getting Started

### What is ProTrain?

ProTrain is BIT Group’s employee training platform. You use it to:

- Study course content and materials
- Complete quizzes and exams
- Track your progress and results
- Compete on leaderboards
- Earn XP (experience points) for learning activities

### How to sign in

| Platform | Where to go |
|----------|-------------|
| **Web** | Open the ProTrain website → **Sign in** (`/auth/login`) |
| **Mobile** | Open the ProTrain app → **Login** |

**You need:**

- Your **work email address**
- Your **password** (provided by your administrator or set during registration)

> **Tip for Zoom training:** Sign in before the session starts so you can follow along on your own device.

### First-time registration (if applicable)

If you received an invitation link or were told to register:

1. Go to **Register** on web or mobile
2. Enter your details and create a password (must meet security requirements)
3. Verify your email if prompted (check your inbox for a verification link)

### After sign-in

You land on your **Home** dashboard with:

- Upcoming exam reminders
- Your stats (attempts, pass rate, training hours)
- Your assigned **courses**
- Available **tests**

## 4. Course Materials

Course materials are the study resources attached to a course (PDFs, videos, images, external links, etc.).

### How to access materials

1. Open a **course**
2. Scroll to the **Course Materials** section
3. Click or tap **Open resource** on any item

Materials are sorted in the order your administrator configured.

### View tracking & XP (mobile)

On the **mobile app**, opening a material for the first time:

- Records your view
- Awards **5 XP** (once per material)

Viewing **all** materials in a course awards a **25 XP** one-time bonus.

> **Note:** On web, materials open in a new tab. View tracking for XP is primarily active on mobile today — still study materials on web; they are the same content.

### Best practice for Zoom training

1. Open your assigned course
2. Walk through 1–2 materials live
3. Confirm everyone can open PDFs/videos on their device
4. Remind learners to review all materials **before** attempting exams

## 6. Tests & Exam Submission

### Before you start

1. Read the **test instructions** carefully
2. Check:
   - **Max attempts** remaining
   - **Time limit** (if the test is timed)
   - **Exam date** (if scheduled)
3. Ensure a stable internet connection

### Step-by-step: taking a test

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Open the test from Home, Course page, or an upcoming exam link |
| 2 | Read instructions → tap/click **Start** |
| 3 | Answer each question (multiple choice and other supported types) |
| 4 | Review your answers if time allows |
| 5 | Click/tap **Submit** |
| 6 | View your score and pass/fail result |

### Timed tests

If a test has a **duration** (e.g. 60 minutes):

- A countdown timer is shown during the attempt
- When time expires, the test **auto-submits**
- Plan your time — do not leave a timed test idle

### Pass mark

You **pass** a test when your score is **60% or higher**.

| Score | Result |
|-------|--------|
| **≥ 60%** | **Passed** |
| **< 60%** | **Failed** — you may retake if attempts remain |

### After submission

- Your result is saved immediately
- You can view it under **Results** / **Past Results**
- XP is awarded for submitting (and extra XP for passing)
- Leaderboard rankings update (where applicable)

### Retaking a test

If you fail and have attempts remaining:

1. Return to the test from the course or home
2. Start a new attempt
3. Only your **latest score per test** counts toward your **Course Knowledge Score**

## 7. Max Attempts

Each test has a **maximum number of attempts** set by your administrator (commonly **1**, but can be higher — e.g. 2, 3, or up to 10).

### What you see

On test cards and test detail screens:

- **Max attempts:** e.g. `3`
- **Attempts used:** e.g. `1 / 3`

### Rules

- Every **non-cancelled** attempt counts toward the limit
- Once you reach the maximum, you **cannot start another attempt**
- You will see: *"Maximum attempts (N) exceeded for this test"*
- If you pass on any attempt, that counts as a pass for course completion

### Important for trainers

- Attempt limits are **per test**, not global
- Different tests in the same course may have different limits
- Plan your study time — treat each attempt seriously, especially when max attempts = 1

## 8. Passing a Course — Which Exams Count

### The rule

A course is **complete** when you have **passed every active Exam** in that course at least once.

| Test type | Counts toward course completion? |
|-----------|----------------------------------|
| **Exam** (active) | **Yes — must pass all** |
| **Quiz** | No |
| **Training** | No |

### What you see

On the course page:

- **"X of Y exams passed"**
- A checklist of mandatory exams with **Passed** / **Not passed** status
- **Course complete** indicator when all exams are passed

### Special cases

- If a course has **no active exams**, it is treated as **100% complete**
- Inactive or deleted exams do not count
- You only need **one passing result** per exam — not a perfect score

### Recommended study order

1. Review **Course Materials** and the **Technical Book**
2. Complete **quizzes/training tests** to build knowledge (optional but helpful)
3. Attempt **exams** when you feel ready
4. Check the mandatory exam checklist until all show **Passed**

## 9. Course Knowledge Score

### What it is

Your **Course Knowledge Score** is a **percentage** showing how well you perform across tests in a course.

It is **not** the same as XP or leaderboard rank — it reflects **test performance only**.

### How it is calculated

```
Knowledge Score = average of your LATEST score on each test you have attempted
```

- Includes **exams, quizzes, and training tests**
- Uses only your **most recent** result per test
- Tests you have never attempted are **excluded**
- Rounded to 2 decimal places (e.g. 72.50%)

### Example

| Test | Latest score | Included? |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Exam 1 | 80% | Yes |
| Exam 2 | 65% | Yes |
| Quiz 1 | 90% | Yes |
| Quiz 2 | (never attempted) | No |

**Knowledge Score = (80 + 65 + 90) ÷ 3 = 78.33%**

### Where to see it

| Platform | Location |
|----------|----------|
| **Web** | Course page → **Knowledge Score** with "Based on N attempted tests" |
| **Mobile** | Course page → **Overall performance** progress card |

### How to improve it

- Retake tests (if attempts remain) to improve your latest score
- Complete more quizzes — each attempted test adds to the average
- Focus on weak areas using course materials before retaking

## 11. Leaderboard

Leaderboards help you see how you compare with colleagues. **Web and mobile show different rankings** — know which one you are viewing.

### Web leaderboard

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Route** | `/leaderboard` |
| **Ranks by** | **Average test score** within a course |
| **Shows** | Rank, name, average score, tests completed |

Rankings are based on your **test performance** in the selected course — higher average score = higher rank.

### Mobile leaderboard

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Route** | **Leaderboard** tab (`/(tabs)/leaderboard`) |
| **Ranks by** | **XP (experience points)** |
| **Filters** | All-time or This month |

Shows your **level** (1–10), **rank tier** (Rookie → Diamond), and XP total.

### How to climb the leaderboard

**Web (course scores):**

- Score well on tests — higher percentages improve your average
- Complete more tests in the course

**Mobile (XP):**

- Log in daily (+10 XP)
- Submit tests (+15 XP), pass tests (+20 XP)
- Pass on first try (+15 bonus XP)
- Score 100% (+40 XP)
- View course materials (+5 XP first time each)
- Complete your profile (+40 XP one-time)

## 12. Email Notifications for Upcoming Tests

ProTrain sends automatic reminders so you do not miss scheduled exams.

### When emails are sent

| Notification | Timing |
|--------------|--------|
| **3-day reminder** | 3 days before the exam date |
| **Exam day reminder** | On the exam date |

Emails are sent daily at **07:00 UTC** when a reminder is due.

### Who receives them

- Learners assigned to the test (via invitation), **or**
- All active learners in the organisation/branch if no specific invitations exist

### When emails are skipped

- You have **already passed** that test
- The test is **inactive**
- No **exam date** is set on the test
- You already received that reminder type for that test

### What the email contains

- Test title and course name
- Exam date
- Link to open the test (web and mobile deep link)
- Reminder of max attempts and pass requirements

### In-app reminders

Even without email, check the **Upcoming exams** banner on:

- Web home
- Mobile home

Shows exams due within the **next 7 days** that you have not yet passed.

**Action:** Click **Take exam** / **View test** from the banner or email link.

## 13. Updating Your Profile & Photo

### Mobile app (recommended for profile updates)

1. Open the **Profile** tab (`/(tabs)/profile`)
2. Tap your **profile photo** or **Edit profile**
3. Update:
   - **First name**
   - **Last name**
   - **Email** (if allowed)
4. To change your photo:
   - Tap the avatar
   - Choose **Photo library**
   - Select an image → it uploads automatically
5. To change your password:
   - Tap **Change password**
   - Enter current password and new password

**Bonus:** Completing your profile (photo + first and last name) awards **40 XP** once.

Pull down to refresh your profile after changes.

### Web app

Profile editing on web is more limited for learners today. If you need to update your name, email, or photo:

- Use the **mobile app** (preferred), **or**
- Contact your **administrator** to update your account

Your avatar appears in the navigation and test interface once set.

## 14. Resetting a Forgotten Password

### Step-by-step

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | On the login screen, tap/click **Forgot password?** |
| 2 | Enter your **registered email address** |
| 3 | Check your inbox for a reset link (also check spam/junk) |
| 4 | Click the link — valid for **15 minutes** |
| 5 | Enter your **new password** and confirm it |
| 6 | Sign in with your new password |

### Routes

| Platform | Forgot password | Reset password |
|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Web** | `/auth/forgot-password` | `/auth/reset-password?token=…` |
| **Mobile** | `/(auth)/forgot-password` | `/(auth)/reset-password` |

### Password requirements

Your new password must:

- Be at least **8 characters**
- Include **uppercase** and **lowercase** letters
- Include at least **one number**
- Include at least **one special character** (e.g. `!@#$%`)

Example format: `ProTrain@2026`

### After reset

- All existing login sessions are signed out
- You receive a **password changed** confirmation email
- Sign in again with your new password

> **Security note:** The reset page always shows a success message when you request a reset — even if the email is not found. This protects account privacy.

## 18. FAQ & Troubleshooting

### I cannot see my course or test

- Confirm you are signed in with the correct **email**
- Your account must be assigned to the correct **organisation/branch**
- The test must be **active** — contact your administrator if missing

### I ran out of attempts

- Each test has a fixed **max attempts** — you cannot exceed it
- Contact your **administrator** if you need an additional attempt (they may reset or increase the limit)

### I passed but the course still shows incomplete

- Only **Exam** type tests count for completion
- Every **active exam** in the course must show **Passed**
- Quizzes and training tests do not count toward completion

### I did not receive a password reset email

- Check **spam/junk** folder
- Confirm you used the **exact email** registered on ProTrain
- Reset links expire in **15 minutes** — request a new one
- Contact your administrator if the problem persists

### My Knowledge Score seems low

- It is the **average of your latest scores** — one low test pulls it down
- Retake tests (if attempts remain) to improve individual scores
- Complete quizzes to add higher scores to the average

### Web vs mobile — which should I use?

- **Web:** Best for Technical Book download, large-screen study, and course score leaderboard
- **Mobile:** Best for profile updates, XP leaderboard, on-the-go testing, and material view XP
- Both access the **same account and results**

### Exam reminder emails

- Sent **3 days before** and **on the day of** the scheduled exam
- You will not receive reminders for tests you have **already passed**
- Also check the **Upcoming exams** banner in the app

### Who do I contact for help?

- **Branch administrator** — access issues, missing courses, attempt resets
- **Trainer / HR** — training schedule and exam deadlines
- **IT support** — app installation, login problems, device compatibility
